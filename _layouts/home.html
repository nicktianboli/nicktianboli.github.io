Tianbo Li

Tianbo Li is currently working as a research scientist at SEA AI Lab, where his primary research area is AI for Quantum Chemistry and Material Science. He obtained his Ph.D from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, under the supervison of Prof. Yiping Ke. Prior to that, he received both his degrees of Bacholer and Master from the School of Statistcs, Renmin University of China. Collaborating closely with Prof. Konstantin Novoselov FRS, Tianbo is actively involved with Institute for Functional Intelligent Materials (I-FIM). Besides his focus on AI for Science, he also has a keen interest in statistical machine learning and exploring stochastic processes for deep learning.
